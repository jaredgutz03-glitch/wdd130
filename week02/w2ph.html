Philippines

Philippines is located in Southeast Asia.The Philippine flag's colors and symbols represent national ideals and history: blue signifies peace, truth, and justice; red symbolizes bravery and patriotism; white represents equality and brotherhood; the sun represents unity, freedom, democracy, and sovereignty; its eight rays honor the first eight provinces that started the revolt against Spain; and the three stars represent the three major island groups: Luzon, Visayas, Mindanao.
