History

Founded in 2005 along the banks of Philippine River, our company began with just a few rafts and a passion for adventure. what started as weekend trips with friends quicly grew into guided tours for families and nature lovers. Over the years, we have expanded to include kayaking and multi-day river expeditions, while always keeping safety,fun, and respect for nature at the heart of what we do. today, we're proud to have guided thousands of adventures through some o the most scenic and exciting waterways in the region.
